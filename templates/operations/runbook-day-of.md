# Day-of Runbook (Template)

Before start
- Confirm registration is open and tech stack is reachable.
- Verify speaker arrivals and AV checks.

During event
- Primary contact and escalation contact listed clearly.
- Track session timing and announce breaks.

Incidents
- Use `incident-brief.md` to capture timeline and impact.
- Escalate to steward if data exposure or PII breach is suspected.

After event
- Collect feedback and publish a short summary.
