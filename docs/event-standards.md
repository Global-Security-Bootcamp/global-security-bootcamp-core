# Event Standards

Program quality
- Hands-on, scenario-driven sessions prioritized.
- Balanced mix of keynote, labs, panels, and practical demos.

Participant experience
- Clear prerequisites and outcomes for each session.
- Accessibility and inclusion considered in scheduling, materials, and delivery.

Operational baseline
- Registration flow and privacy statements.
- Day-of runbook and escalation paths.
- Post-event feedback and retention guidance.
