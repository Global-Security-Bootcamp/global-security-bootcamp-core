# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

Reporting
- If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the event lead or email conduct@your-org.example.
- Maintain confidentiality and follow local escalation policies.

Enforcement
- Reports are reviewed by the stewardship team. Remedial actions may include warnings, removal from the event, and banning from future events.
